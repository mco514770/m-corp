when do you bake your cookies?
<\/span><\/h1>\n

we bake our cookies fresh from our bakery everyday. we continuously bake throughout the day until our last hour of operation to keep cookies fresh. at the end of the day, we donate all leftover cookies to a local food bank or use too good to go to eliminate food waste. and the next day, we do it all over again.<\/span><\/span><\/h1>\n

do your cookies contain nuts and other allergens?
<\/span><\/h1>\n

Our cookies may contain OR HAVE COME IN CONTACT WITH PEANUTS, TREE nuts, DAIRY, wheat, EGG and soy. While not all cookies have nuts and other allergens as ingredients, we make all the cookies in the same place so there is potential for cross-contamination. We cannot guarantee that any of our flavours will be nut free. please ask us any questions regarding any allergy concerns and we’ll be happy to answer.<\/span><\/span><\/h1>\n

HOW do i reheat the cookies?
<\/span><\/h1>\n

Our cookies are best enjoyed warm. The cookies can be reheated for about 10-15 seconds in the microwave or for 3-5 minutes in a 350\u00b0F oven or until warmed through. You can also reheat frozen cookies in a 350\u00b0F oven for 8-10 minutes or until warmed through.<\/span><\/span><\/h1>\n
